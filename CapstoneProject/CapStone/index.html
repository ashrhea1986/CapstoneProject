Our Story

Growing up Paul was always intrigued by anything electronic. From playing video games to taking apart old electronics and putting them back together he did it all. He always dreamed of having his own gaming store and so Entertainment Shack was created. Entertainment Shack began in 2009 as a small store in Edmonton, Ky. It was later moved to a location in Glasgow, Ky. As business grew they relocated to a larger building in Glasgow where they operated until making the decision to go online only.

They currently operate completely online using Ebay and Paypal to make shopping easy on all customers.
